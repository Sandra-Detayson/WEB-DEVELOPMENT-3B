Founding and Early Years

Establishment: GreenHarvest was founded in 1989 with the vision of offering fresh, locally-sourced vegetables and fruits to the community. The founders aimed to provide high-quality produce while supporting local farmers and promoting healthy eating.

Initial Offerings:

The store began with a modest selection of fresh produce and gradually expanded its range as it gained popularity among customers.
